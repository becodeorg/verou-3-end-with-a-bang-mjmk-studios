Polyphonic Synthesizer

Press the keys on your keyboard to play the sound. You can play multiple sounds and play upper or lower octaves with letter 'w' or 'x'. The keys are set up for azerty keyboard layout.

  • The white keys are: q, s, d, f, g, h, j, k, l, m, ù, µ.
  • The black keys are: z, e, t, y, u, o, p, $.
